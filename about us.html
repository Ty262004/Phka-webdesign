About Phka Beauty

Embracing Cambodian elegance through beauty products inspired by nature's most delicate blossoms

Our Story

Phka Beauty was born from a deep appreciation for Cambodia's rich floral heritage and the vision of founder Pich Pisey. The name "Phka," meaning flower in Khmer, reflects our commitment to capturing the essence of Cambodia's native blossoms in every product.

What started as a passion for natural beauty has blossomed into a brand that celebrates Cambodian femininity, elegance, and the gentle power of nature's palette.

Founded with love and passion

Our Mission

To create beauty products that honor Cambodian heritage while embracing modern elegance. We strive to empower individuals to express their unique beauty through products inspired by nature's most delicate creations.

Our Vision

To become Cambodia's leading beauty brand, recognized globally for our commitment to quality, cultural authenticity, and sustainable practices that celebrate the beauty of our natural world.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Phka Beauty

Natural Ingredients

We carefully select natural ingredients inspired by Cambodia's rich flora, ensuring our products are gentle yet effective.

Cultural Heritage

Each product tells a story of Cambodian culture, tradition, and the timeless beauty of our heritage.

Quality & Elegance

We combine traditional knowledge with modern innovation to create products of exceptional quality and elegance.
